Social insurance number

Important: Your social insurance number is required in order for the college to produce your tax receipts for Quebec (Relevé 8) and Canada (T2202). These documents are necessary in order to complete your income tax form or your parents income tax forms. To provide this information, go to Omnivox and click on « Personnal File ».

Deadline to drop a class without a failure

Please note that you have until 4:00 p.m. on Friday, February 12, 2021 to drop a class. Anything after this date will result in a failure on your transcript. In order to drop a class, you need to meet with your Academic Advisor to fill out the appropriate paperwork. This deadline applies to students who are following a regular training in a DEC program.
